📖 Rule Book

🎯 Objective

Be the last player standing! Buy properties, collect rent, and bankrupt your opponents.

💰 Starting

Each player starts with Rs.50,000 and a token on START.

🎲 Playing

  • Roll dice and move your token clockwise
  • Pass START → Collect Rs.2,000
  • Land on property → Buy it or Auction
  • Land on owned property → Pay rent

🎴 CHANCE Cards (by dice total)

EVEN Numbers = PAY:

  • #2 — Inflation → Pay Rs.200
  • #4 — Fire insurance → Pay Rs.2,000
  • #6 — Share loss → Pay Rs.1,500
  • #8 — Drunk driving → Pay Rs.3,000
  • #10 — Donation → Pay Rs.1,000
  • #12 — Parking fine → Pay Rs.500

ODD Numbers = COLLECT (except #7):

  • #3 — Lottery → Collect Rs.2,000
  • #5 — TV prize → Collect Rs.3,000
  • #7 — 🔒 GO TO PRISON!
  • #9 — Dividend → Collect Rs.1,000
  • #11 — Inheritance → Collect Rs.4,000

🤝 COMMUNITY CHEST Cards (by dice total)

EVEN Numbers = COLLECT:

  • #2 — Birthday → Rs.1,000 from EACH player
  • #4 — Tax refund → Collect Rs.4,000
  • #6 — Interest → Collect Rs.1,000
  • #8 — Stock sale → Collect Rs.6,000
  • #10 — Game show → Collect Rs.5,000
  • #12 — Bonus → Collect Rs.2,000

ODD Numbers = PAY:

  • #3 — Insurance → Pay Rs.200
  • #5 — Donation → Pay Rs.1,000
  • #7 — Consultancy → Pay Rs.1,500
  • #9 — Repairs → Pay Rs.500
  • #11 — Hospital → Pay Rs.2,000

🏠🏨 Building Houses & Hotels

IMPORTANT: You must own ALL cities in a color group to build! Maximum 3 houses (🏠 red) and 1 hotel (🏨 black) per city. Each costs 50% of property price.

🚂 Utilities

Railways, Airways, Roadways, Waterways, Electric & Gas Co. — Rent is 50% of the purchase price.

🔒 Prison

  • Roll doubles to escape (3 attempts)
  • Or pay Rs.500 fine

🎰 Club

Landing here means you pay Rs.100 to each other player + Rs.100 to the bank.

🏡 Rest House

Landing here means you collect Rs.100 from each other player + Rs.100 from the bank and skip your next turn.

🏦 Bank Loan

ONE-TIME ONLY! Borrow up to Rs.20,000. No interest! Must repay at game end to win!

💸 Taxes

  • Income Tax: Pay Rs.100
  • Wealth Tax: Pay Rs.100
